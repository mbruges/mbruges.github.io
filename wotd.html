The Word of The Day is FASCINATE 👌

Fascinate (verb) - to attract and hold the attention or interest of someone.

Etymology: from Latin fascinare meaning 'to bewitch, enchant'.

Example sentences:

  • The lecture on ancient Egypt fascinated me.
  • The magician's tricks never fail to fascinate his audience.

Practice tasks:

  1. Write a paragraph about something that fascinates you.
  2. Find three synonyms for fascinate and write them down.
  3. Use the word fascinate in a sentence that includes the word 'curious'.

Creative writing:

Write a short story about a character who is fascinated by something that others find boring or unimportant.

Drawing task:

Draw a picture of something that fascinates you.
