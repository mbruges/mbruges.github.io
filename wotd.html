The Word of The Day is VORACIOUS 🤭

Voracious (adj.): Having a huge appetite, excessively eager.

Etymology: From Latin vorax ("devouring")

Example sentences:

  • The voracious puppy ate its entire bowl of food in seconds.
  • He has a voracious appetite for knowledge and always reads books.

Practice tasks:

  1. Write a sentence using voracious.
  2. Find a synonym for voracious.
  3. Use voracious in a sentence that describes your own eating habits.

Creative writing task:

Write a short story about a voracious animal that wreaks havoc on a small town.

Drawing task:

Draw a picture of a hungry monster with a voracious appetite.
