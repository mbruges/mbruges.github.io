The Word of The Day is... Obfuscate 🕵️‍♀️

Definition: To make something obscure or unclear, usually by making it more complicated or confusing.

Example Sentences

  1. The terms and conditions were deliberately obfuscated to prevent people from understanding them.
  2. The politician's answers to the reporters were obfuscated by their vague language and use of jargon.

Practice Tasks

  1. How can one obfuscate a simple message?
  2. Give an example of a situation where obfuscation might be useful.
  3. What measures can one take to prevent obfuscation in communication?

Open-Ended Creative Writing Task

Write a story about a spy who has to obfuscate their mission in order to avoid detection.

Drawing Task

Draw a scene where something is obfuscated or hidden.
