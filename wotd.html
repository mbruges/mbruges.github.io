The Word of The Day is PHENOMENOLOGY 🔎

Phenomenology is the philosophical study of experiences from the perspective of the person experiencing them. It is a method of inquiry that focuses on the nature and structure of consciousness.

The word phenomenology derives from the Greek words "phainomenon" meaning "thing appearing" and "logos" meaning "study," so its literal meaning is "the study of appearing things."
