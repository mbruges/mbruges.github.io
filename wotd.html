The Word of The Day is ONTOSIS 📑️

ONTOSIS (noun) - the process of becoming one with something else through assimilation or absorption

Etymology: The word ONTOSIS comes from the Greek word 'ontos', which means being or existence.

  1. His complete ontosiswith the culture of Paris was surprising.
  2. The ontosis of social media into our daily lives is undeniable.
