The Word of The Day is RIGOROUS 🔥

RIGOROUS (adjective): extremely thorough, careful, and precise

Etymology: from Latin rigorosus, from rigor (stiffness)

Example Sentences:

1. The rigorous training program prepared him well for the competition.

2. She gave a rigorous analysis of the data.

Practice Tasks:

  1. Write a short paragraph describing a time when you had to be rigorous.
  2. Make a list of ten synonyms for rigorous.
  3. Underline all the instances of rigorous in this sentence: "The teacher was impressed with the student's rigorous approach to the assignment."

Creative Writing Task:

Write a short story about a scientist who must be rigorous in their experiments to save the world from a deadly virus.

Drawing Task:

Draw a picture of a person who is being rigorous in their studies or work.
