The Word of The Day is... Ebullient üî•

Ebullient (adj.): cheerful and full of energy

Etymology: The word 'ebullient' comes from the Latin word 'ebullire' which means 'to boil over'.

Example Sentences

  1. After receiving the good news, she became ebullient.
  2. The children were ebullient at the prospect of going to Disneyland.

Practice Tasks

  • Write a sentence using ebullient to describe a person you know.

Creative Writing Task

Write a short story about an ebullient person on their journey to achieve their dreams. Include details about their personality, their struggles, and how their optimism helps them overcome obstacles.

Drawing Task

Draw a picture of a person who is ebullient. Don't forget to show their joyful and energetic personality through the details in your artwork.
