The Word of The Day is BENEVOLENCE 🌟

Benevolence is the quality of being kind, helpful, and generous towards others.

The word benevolence comes from the Latin words ben (meaning "well") and volentia (meaning "wishing").

Example sentences:

  • She showed great benevolence towards her neighbors by cooking meals for them during the lockdown.
  • His benevolence towards animals led him to start a shelter for homeless pets.

Practice tasks:

  1. Write a sentence using benevolence.
  2. Find a synonym for benevolence.
  3. Make a list of three ways you can show benevolence to someone today.

Open-ended creative writing task:

Write a short story about a character who goes out of their way to help others, and how their acts of benevolence impact those around them.

Drawing task:

Draw a picture of someone showing benevolence towards others.
