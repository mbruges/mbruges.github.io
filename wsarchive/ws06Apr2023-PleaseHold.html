Reflection Grid

What is the poem describing? What themes are explored?
    What questions would you ask?     What comparisons could be made?

Part 1: Comprehension Questions

Read the poem 'Please Hold' by Ciaran O'Driscoll and answer the following questions:

  1. What is the speaker waiting for?
  2. What does the speaker compare the hold music to?
  3. What does the speaker say about their current state of mind?
  4. What is the overall tone of the poem?

Part 2: Textual Analysis

Using your understanding of the poem, answer the following questions:

  1. What is the significance of the title 'Please Hold'?
  2. What is the effect of the repetition of the phrase "please hold" throughout the poem?
  3. What is the significance of the final line of the poem?
  4. What literary devices are used in the poem? Provide examples.
  5. What is the overall message or theme of the poem?

Part 3: Critical Analysis

Using your analysis of the poem, develop your own critical arguments about its meaning and significance. Answer the following questions:

  1. How does the poem relate to larger societal or cultural issues?
  2. What emotions does the poem evoke in you, and why do you believe those emotions are elicited?
  3. What is your personal interpretation of the poem, and how does it differ from other interpretations you've heard or read?
