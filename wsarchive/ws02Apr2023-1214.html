Writing Formal Letters

Formal letters are written for official purposes such as to request information, to complain, to apply for a job, etc. These letters follow specific rules and conventions.

Example Formal Letter

Below is an example of a formal letter. Work in pairs to annotate and highlight the key features such as the salutation, date, and main body. Then discuss why each of these features is important in a formal letter.

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing to inquire about your company's internship programme. I am a student at ABC University studying Business Administration and I am interested in gaining some practical experience in this field.

Could you please provide me with some information about your internship programme, such as the requirements, duration, and application process? I would be grateful for any details you could provide.

Thank you for your time.

Yours faithfully,

John Smith

Brainstorming Task

Think of a situation in which you might need to write a formal letter. Together, write down some ideas for the content and language conventions that you should use in this letter.

Research Task

Choose a company or organization that you are interested in or have a connection to. Research what types of formal letters they might receive and what language conventions they use. Summarize your findings and discuss why it is important to know these conventions when writing a formal letter.
