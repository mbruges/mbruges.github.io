I am the poetry of T.S. Eliot

T.S. Eliot was a famous poet who wrote several important poems in the modernist tradition. Some of his most well-known works include "The Love Song of J. Alfred Prufrock," "The Waste Land," and "Four Quartets."

Key Terms

  • Modernism
  • Stream of consciousness
  • Fragmentation
  • Allusion

Quick Questions

  1. What are some of T.S. Eliot's most famous works?
  2. What is modernism?
  3. What is stream of consciousness?
  4. What is fragmentation?

Task

Choose one of the characters in "The Waste Land" and write a short paragraph from their perspective. How do they feel? What are they experiencing? How do they fit into the larger narrative of the poem?

Research Task

Research the connections between T.S. Eliot's life and his poetry. How did his personal experiences influence his writing? What were his literary influences? How did he contribute to the modernist movement?
