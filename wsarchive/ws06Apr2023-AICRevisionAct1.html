Comprehension Questions

  1. Who is the first character to be questioned by the Inspector?
  2. What is the purpose of the Inspector's visit?
  3. What is the relationship between Sheila Birling and Gerald Croft?
  4. What is Mr Birling's attitude towards the Inspector's questions?

  1. Arthur Birling
  2. To investigate the suicide of a young girl named Eva Smith/Daisy Renton
