Volcanoes Worksheet

Brief Summary of Key Terms

  • Volcano - an opening in the Earth's surface where lava, ash, and gas can escape
  • Magma - molten rock beneath the Earth's surface
  • Lava - molten rock above the Earth's surface
  • Pyroclastic flow - a fast-moving current of hot gas and volcanic matter that flows along the ground
  • Ash - small fragments of volcanic rock and glass that can travel long distances from a volcanic eruption
  • Volcanic bomb - a large, solid piece of volcanic rock that is ejected during an eruption
