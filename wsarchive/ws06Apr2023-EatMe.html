Section 1: Reflection Grid

Read the poem and fill in the quadrant grid below:

What is the poem describing? What themes are explored?
    What questions would you ask?     What comparisons could be made?

Section 2: Comprehension Questions

  1. What is the title of the poem?
  2. What is the tone of the poem?
  3. Who is the speaker of the poem?
  4. What is the meaning of "the bigger the better" in the context of the poem?

Section 3: Textual Language Analysis

  1. Identify and explain any use of figurative language in the poem.
  2. What is the effect of using informal language in the poem?

Section 4: Critical Thinking

Develop your own critical arguments about the poem:

  • What do you think is the poet's message in this poem?
  • How does the use of repetition contribute to the overall meaning of the poem?
  • Do you think the poem is effective in conveying its message? Why or why not?
