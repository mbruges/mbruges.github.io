Brief Summary of Key Terms:

  • Macbeth: The main protagonist of the play who is a Scottish nobleman and a warrior.
  • Duncan: The King of Scotland who is murdered by Macbeth in his quest for power.
  • Witches: Three supernatural beings who prophesize Macbeth's rise to power and downfall.
  • Banquo: A Scottish nobleman and Macbeth's friend who is also prophesized to become the father of kings.

Short Questions:

  1. Who is the main protagonist of the play?
  2. Who is the King of Scotland who is murdered?
  3. What is the role of the witches in the play?
  4. Who is Banquo and what is his significance?
