Key Terms

  • Egg - The beginning stage of a butterfly's life
  • Larva - The caterpillar stage, where the caterpillar eats and grows
  • Pupa - The stage where the caterpillar transforms into a butterfly within a chrysalis
  • Adult - The final stage where the butterfly emerges from the chrysalis and can fly and reproduce

Creativity Task

Using paint and glue, create a poster or model of the life-cycle of a butterfly. Include all four stages and label each stage. Be creative and make it colorful!

Open-ended Research Task

Research different types of butterflies and their habitats. Create a presentation or report that includes pictures, interesting facts, and information about where these butterflies live and what they eat.
