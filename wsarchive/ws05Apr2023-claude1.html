Key terms: headline, lead paragraph, facts, accuracy, balance, impartiality, quotes, attribution

  1. What is the purpose of a news headline?
  2. What should the lead paragraph contain?
  3. Why must news reports be factual?
  4. What is meant by balance and impartiality in the media?

Analysis task: Read the article attached. Assess how well it fulfils the key characteristics of a news report. Identify any ways it could be improved.

Writing task: Write a news report on a local event in your area. Aim for 3 short paragraphs for the full report.

Research task: Investigate how news stories are selected and prioritised for publication. What factors determine if a story becomes headline news?

Present your findings to the class.
