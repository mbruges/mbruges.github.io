The Word of The Day is ABERRATION 👽

Aberration: a departure from what is normal, usual, or expected, typically one that is unwelcome.

Derived from the Latin word "aberratio", meaning a wandering or straying away from. The word was first recorded in the mid 16th century.

Example Sentences

  • Her decision to drop out of college was an aberration; she had always been an excellent student.
  • The sudden rush of customers was an aberration; the store was usually empty.
  • His behavior at the party was an aberration; normally he was reserved and polite.
