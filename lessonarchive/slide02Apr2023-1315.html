Exploring the Fury of Volcanoes

What happens when a volcano erupts?

Do Now: Write down everything you know about volcanoes. Share one interesting fact with a classmate.

Key Terms:

  • Magma - Molten rock beneath the Earth's surface
  • Lava - Magma that has erupted onto the Earth's surface
  • Pyroclastic flow - A fast-moving current of hot gas and volcanic matter that flows from an erupting volcano
  • Crater - The depression at the summit of a volcano where eruptions occur
  • Caldera - A large crater caused by the collapse of the top of a volcano
